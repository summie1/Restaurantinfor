About

MongoDB is a source-available cross-platform document-oriented database program. Classified as a NoSQL database program, A record in MongoDB is a document, which is a data structure composed of field and value pairs. MongoDB documents are similar to JSON objects. The values of fields may include other documents, arrays, and arrays of documents..
